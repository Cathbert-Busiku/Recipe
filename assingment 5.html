The Easiest Panckake Recipe Ever

As Perfected by Our Test Kitchen

in just 30 minutes , whip up a crowd pleasing breakfast (or dinner) from scratch.

A pancake is a flat, often thin and round, prepared from a stratch-based batter that may contain eggs,milk and butter and cooked on hot surface such as griddle or frying pan. As for me a breakfast without a pancakes is not a breakfast, i really enjoy the hot delicate, yummy, delicious taste of pancakes. just their spice smell is unresistable.

Ingredients

  • all-purpose flour
  • tbsp.sugar
  • 1/2 tsp.baking powder
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • tbsp.butter,melted
  • 1 large egg
  • vegetable oil for brushing pan

Directions

  1. In large bowl, whisk flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Then add 🍲
    • milk 🍶
    • butter
    • egg ; 🥚
    stir until flour is moistened 🍽
  2. Heat 12-inch nonstick skillet or griddle over medium heat until drop of water sizzles.;
    1. brush slightly with oil,
    2. In batches,scoop batter by scant 1/4-cupfuls into skillet,
    3. Cook 2 to 3 munites or until bubbly and edges are dry, the cook 2 munites more until golden 🍳
    4. Transfer to platter or keep warm on a cookie sheet in 225F oven,otherwise they are ready to save 😋.
  3. Repeat with remaining batter, brushing griddle with more oil if necessary 🍩

when the pancake are done cooking they look golden blown. A warm, buttery and sweet smell flows though the air as hot, blueberry flecked pancakes reach the plates which they will be eaten on. Butter slides slowly down the pancakes, leaving a a think yellow trail behind it. :D This is the simplest and quickest way to preapare yourself a nice pancake breakfast.
